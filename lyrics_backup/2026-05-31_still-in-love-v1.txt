[RAW LYRICS]
[Hook]
Shadows dancing slow across the hardwood floor
I’m watching the way that your body sways
I want to feel the heat of your skin once more
And kiss the rest of the world away

[Verse 1]
We left the porch light burning hours ago
Lost in the heavy, sweet midnight breeze
I’m watching the shadows moving so slow
While you bring me down to my knees

[Pre-Chorus]
The firelight is glowing so softly
And I’m pulling you into my space
I’m letting my guard down completely
To trace every line on your face

[Chorus]
I’m still in love with the way that you look at me
Like we’re the only two souls awake
You are the fire and the thrill in my memory
Every single heartbeat I take

[Post-Chorus]
Yeah, I’m still falling, still falling
Deeper with every breath you’re drawing

[Verse 2]
There’s a rustle of leaves just outside the door
But I’m tuned to the rhythm of your sigh
I’ve never craved someone like this before
Beneath this open, endless sky

[Pre-Chorus 2]
The firelight is glowing so softly
And I’m pulling you into my space
I’m letting my guard down completely
Lost in the warmth of your embrace

[Chorus]
I’m still in love with the way that you look at me
Like we’re the only two souls awake
You are the fire and the thrill in my memory

[Post-Chorus 2]
Yeah, I’m still falling, still falling
Deeper with every breath you’re drawing

[Bridge]
We could watch the morning light paint the pines
Or let the shadows keep us blind
As long as your body is pressing to mine
I’m leaving the lonely miles behind
I swear Saeed will never let you down
I’m dropping my anchor in your quiet town

[Spoken]
